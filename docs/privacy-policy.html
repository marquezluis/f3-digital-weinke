Digital Weinke — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Digital Weinke ("the app") is an unofficial companion app for F3 Nation, built to help PAX plan, run, and log F3 workouts (beatdowns). This page explains what the app collects, why, and where it goes.

What the app collects

DataWhyWhere it goes
F3 Nation identity (name, email, phone if provided) Signing in with your existing F3 Nation account Authenticated directly with F3 Nation's own servers (auth2.f3nation.com) via OAuth. Digital Weinke never sees your password.
Precise location (while the app is open) Sorting nearby AOs on the Browse AOs map Stays on your device — used only to sort/center the map. Never transmitted or stored anywhere.
Profile photo Displaying your avatar in the app Stored only on your device. Never uploaded.
Emergency info (blood type, allergies, conditions, medications, emergency contact) Available on-screen in an emergency, even without signing in Stored only on your device, encrypted (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). Never transmitted anywhere.
Workout history, plans, and settings So the app remembers what you've done and how you like it configured Stored only on your device.
Schedule, attendance, and backblast data you choose to publish Posting your Q credit, attendance, and backblasts back to F3 Nation Sent to F3 Nation's own API (api.f3nation.com) — this is F3 Nation's system of record, not a third party.
Messages you send to the Spartan AI assistant Getting Q-prep suggestions and workout ideas Sent to Google's Generative AI API (Gemini) to generate a response. Not stored by Digital Weinke.

What we don't do

No ads, no analytics trackers, no selling or sharing data with data brokers, no background location tracking. Biometric unlock (Face ID / Touch ID / fingerprint) is handled entirely by your device's operating system — Digital Weinke never receives or stores any biometric data itself.

Your controls

You can sign out of your F3 Nation account at any time from Settings, which removes your stored sign-in tokens from the device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including workout history, emergency info, and your profile photo.

Children's privacy

Digital Weinke is intended for adult F3 Nation members and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If what the app collects changes, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
